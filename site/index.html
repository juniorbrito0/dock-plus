The idea

Your Dock holds apps. Dock+ holds information.

The macOS Dock launches things. Dock+ adds a second, glanceable strip of live tiles next to it — the stuff you keep cmd-tabbing to check. It floats above everything, follows you across every Space, and gently lifts out of the way when an auto-hidden Dock reveals.

🪟

Floats beside the Dock

A borderless glass panel pinned to your chosen screen edge, above every window, on all Spaces. It never steals focus and never shows up in cmd-tab.

⚡

Live, not static

Weather, now-playing, CPU, battery, your next meeting and today's reminders refresh on their own. The electric-teal accent lights up only when a tile is active or live.

🔒

On-device by default

No account, no analytics, no telemetry. The only thing that ever leaves your Mac is your approximate location, sent to Open-Meteo for the weather — and nothing else.

🎛️

Yours to arrange

Toggle any of the 15 widgets on or off, pick the screen edge, and add your favorite apps. Keep a tight three-tile dock or run the full set.

🤏

Discreet minimize

Collapse the whole bar to a slim handle when you want the screen back. One click brings it home.

🍎

Native & light

A small SwiftUI menu-bar app built for macOS 14 Sonoma. No Electron, no background bloat — it reads the system directly.

Widgets

Fifteen widgets. One glance.

Each tile does one useful thing well — and many are interactive, not just read-only. Control music, archive mail, paste an emoji, or copy a password without leaving the Dock.
