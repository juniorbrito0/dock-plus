The catalog

Fifteen widgets,
all on one bar

Mix and match. Each widget is a single tile that does one thing well — and many let you act, not just look. Tags show what runs on-device versus the one widget that makes a network call.

🕘

Clock Local

Time and date at a glance, formatted for your locale. Nothing to configure — it just keeps time.

🌤️

Weather Network

Current local conditions and temperature, powered by the keyless Open-Meteo API. Uses your approximate location — no API key, no account.

🎧

Music Local

Now-playing artwork, play/pause and skip controls, plus your current playlist in a popover. Works with Apple Music and Spotify (Spotify shows now-playing; full playlist listing is Music.app).

📅

Calendar Local

Your next event with smart urgency color-coding — calm when it's hours away, amber as it approaches, red when it's now. Read locally from your Mac's calendars.

✉️

Mail Local

The last three messages in your inbox. Archive or delete inline straight from the tile, so you can triage without opening Mail.

📊

System Local

Live CPU and RAM usage, read directly from the kernel. A quiet pulse when your Mac is working hard.

🔋

Battery Local

Charge level and charging state via IOKit. Know at a glance whether you can wander off without the charger.

🚀

Apps Local

A favorite-app launcher right on the bar. Pin the apps you open all day and launch them with one click.

📁

Folders Local

Pinned folders with QuickLook file previews. Peek inside a project or your Downloads without opening Finder.

🔖

Bookmarks Local

A drop zone for links. Drag a URL onto the tile to save it, then open your shortcuts with a click.

😀

Emoji Local

Your top-5 most-used emoji plus a full picker. Click one and Dock+ pastes it straight into whatever app you're focused on.

✦

Ask Claude Local

One click opens a fresh chat in the Claude desktop app. Your shortcut to asking, drafting or debugging without hunting for the window.

🔑

1Password Local

Search your vault and copy a password to the clipboard — authorized with Touch ID through the local op CLI. Nothing is transmitted, and the clipboard auto-clears after 90 seconds.

⚙️

Quick Actions Local

One-tap system shortcuts — take a screenshot, sleep the display, and more. The small chores you keep digging through menus for.

🧭

Dia Tabs Local

See your open tabs in the Dia browser and jump straight to any one — right from the bar.
